Come Learn With Us!

Reading In the Park (RITP) is a community-driven club dedicated to fostering learning, creativity, and connection through shared reading and discussion in the great outdoors. Every other Friday, we gather for 2–2.5 hours at Plainfield’s Village Green (with hopes to expand!) to enjoy books, exchange ideas, and inspire one another. Our goal is to make knowledge and creativity accessible while strengthening community bonds—whether through literature, art, or collaborative projects. Currently, we’re seeking funding to provide transportation for those who need it, ensuring everyone can participate. Long-term, we aim to empower others to launch their own local reading and learning initiatives. Join us—let’s grow together, one page at a time!
